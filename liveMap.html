The Live Map

The live map shows which places are currently affected by disasters. To help people understand which areas are safe, the map has color coded areas that are safe, cautious, or dangerous. The map will be updated, so people have the latest information available.

  • Red: Danger
  • Yellow/Orange: Proceed with Caution
  • Green: Safe
